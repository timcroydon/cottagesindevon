About Our Holiday Home

Our holiday home "Carpenters" is situated in the friendly village of Combe Martin on the North Devon coast. Carpenters is a luxury quality bungalow situated less that 100 yards from the beach, and within an easy walk of the local shops. It is ideal for a holiday break with your children.

About Combe Martin

Combe Martin, situated in a region designated as an area of “Outstanding Natural Beauty” on the English Heritage North Devon Coast, and on the edge of Exmoor National Park, is the ideal base for all types of holidays. Castle Street forms part of what is thought to be the second longest village street in the country. There are a variety of shops and 6 pubs along the length of the village, including the famous 17th Century Pack ‘O Cards. There are many footpaths to explore together with beaches, the harbour and the South West Coastal Path.

Combe Martin Wildlife & Dinosaur Park is situated within easy reach of the village.

Also worth visiting are the beaches at Woolacombe (Blue Flag), Saunton Sands and Croyde (all suitable for surfing); the historic harbour town of Ilfracombe; the market town of Barnstaple (its Pannier Market is always worth a visit), Wistlandpound Reservoir; Lynton and Lynmouth. Details of golf courses, bowling greens, walks and other activities and attractions are available at the Combe Martin Tourist Information Centre situated near the harbour.
